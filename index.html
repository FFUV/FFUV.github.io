Robert J. Oppenheimer

The visionary mind behind the Little Dark Age

About Oppenheimer

Robert J. Oppenheimer was a brilliant physicist and the scientific director of the Manhattan Project, leading to the creation of the atomic bomb. His complex life and contributions to science are a testament to the duality of human achievement.

Little Dark Age

"Little Dark Age" is a term used to describe Oppenheimer's era, marked by the development of destructive forces. It reflects the somber undertones of scientific advancements and the moral questions they pose.
